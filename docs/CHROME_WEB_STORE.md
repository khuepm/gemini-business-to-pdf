## Permissions Justification

### activeTab
**Why needed**: To access the current Gemini Business tab and extract chat content for PDF generation.

**User benefit**: Allows the extension to read and process your conversation only when you click the export button.

### host_permissions (https://gemini.google.com/*)
**Why needed**: To inject the export button and content script into Gemini Business pages.

**User benefit**: Enables seamless integration with Gemini Business interface.
